Ghana

Ghana is a country in West Africa. It is bordered by Togo to the east, Cote D'Ivoire to the west and Bukina Faso to the North. It is known for it's industrial minerals like gold, bauxite, manganese, and limestone.

Ghana is also known for its rich culture, diverse wildlife, and vibrant cities. The capital city, Accra, is a bustling metropolis with a mix of modern and traditional influences. The country has a strong sense of community and hospitality, making it a welcoming destination for visitors.

Ghana is also home to several UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the Cape Coast Castle and the Ashanti Traditional Buildings in Kumasi. The country has a rich history, with influences from various cultures and civilizations, making it a unique and fascinating place to explore.
